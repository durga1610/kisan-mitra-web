Vegetative: Apply NPK 19-19-19 or Urea at 25 kg/acre.
Flowering: Apply Boron and DAP (Diammonium Phosphate) at 30 kg/acre.
Fruiting: Apply Potassium Sulphate (SOP) or MOP at 25 kg/acre.
Dosage: 25 kg/acre
Method: Soil application near root zone followed by immediate irrigation.
Precautions: Do not apply fertilizer directly on the foliage to avoid leaf scorch.
