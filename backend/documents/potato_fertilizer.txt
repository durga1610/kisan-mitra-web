Vegetative: Apply DAP at 50 kg/acre and Ammonium Sulphate at 30 kg/acre.
Flowering: Apply Urea at 25 kg/acre during earthing-up stage.
Fruiting: Apply Potassium Chloride (MOP) at 40 kg/acre for tuber bulking.
Dosage: 50 kg/acre
Method: Placement in bands on both sides of the potato ridge.
Precautions: Avoid excessive nitrogen close to harvest to prevent watery tubers.
