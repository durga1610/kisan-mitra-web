Vegetative: Apply Urea at 30 kg/acre and Zinc Sulphate at 10 kg/acre.
Flowering: Apply DAP at 40 kg/acre at tillering stage.
Fruiting: Apply MOP (Muriate of Potash) at 20 kg/acre.
Dosage: 30 kg/acre
Method: Broadcast evenly across the standing water in the paddy field.
Precautions: Maintain a thin water layer (2-5 cm) when broadcasting fertilizer.
