=== HEMP CROP GUIDE ===

1. Fertilizers:
Hemp requires balanced nutrient applications. Recommended dose is 80:40:80 kg of N:P:K per acre (or per tree/bush equivalent).
Category: Plantation Crops
- Apply fertilizer in two splits: post-monsoon (October) and pre-monsoon (May).
- Apply organic manure and oil cakes annually to enrich the root basin.
- Foliar spray secondary nutrients and trace elements to boost leaf/stem growth.

2. Irrigation:
Hemp irrigation and water requirements: High water requirement. Regularly irrigate, especially during dry months. Ensure excellent drainage.

3. Pest Management:
Hemp pest management: Common pests affecting Hemp are White Stem Borer, Berry Borer, Mealybugs. Install traps and use organic biological controls.

4. Growth Stages:
Hemp has the following stage cycle durations:
- Vegetative Stage: Sowing up to 120 days.
- Flowering Stage: From 120 days to 240 days.
- Fruiting Stage: From 240 days to 300 days.
- Harvesting Stage: From 300 days onwards.

5. Harvesting:
Hemp harvesting and post-harvest practices: For Hemp, Harvest according to commodity guidelines (e.g., plucking buds/leaves, gathering pods, or nuts).

6. Diseases:
Hemp diseases: Common diseases affecting Hemp are Leaf Rust, Root Rot, Blight. Prune and spray copper oxychloride or systemic fungicides.

7. Soil Requirements:
Hemp soil requirements: Requires deep, well-drained forest loam, laterite, or alluvial soil. Optimal pH is 5.0 to 6.5.
