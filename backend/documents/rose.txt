=== ROSE CROP GUIDE ===

1. Fertilizers:
Rose requires balanced nutrient applications. Recommended dose is 40:40:40 kg of N:P:K per acre.
Category: Plantation Crops
- Apply well-rotted cow manure (FYM) post-pruning.
- Apply NPK split doses during vegetative and bud initiation stages.
- Apply magnesium sulphate and micronutrients to improve flower size and color.

2. Irrigation:
Rose irrigation and water requirements: Moderate water requirement. Irrigate twice a week in winter, and every alternate day in summer. Drip irrigation keeps foliage dry, preventing fungal diseases.

3. Pest Management:
Rose pest management: Thrips, Aphids, and Red Spider Mites. Thrips cause brown scarring on flower petals. Control by spraying neem oil (5ml/L) or imidacloprid.

4. Growth Stages:
Rose has the following stage cycle durations:
- Vegetative Stage: Sowing up to 30 days.
- Flowering Stage: From 30 days to 60 days.
- Fruiting Stage: From 60 days to 90 days.
- Harvesting Stage: From 90 days onwards.

5. Harvesting:
Rose harvesting and post-harvest practices: Harvest in the cool early morning or late evening. Cut flowers at the tight bud stage when color starts showing. Place stems immediately in clean water.

6. Diseases:
Rose diseases: Black Spot, Powdery Mildew, and Dieback. Dieback causes blackening of branches downwards from pruned ends. Apply copper oxychloride paste to pruned tips.

7. Soil Requirements:
Rose soil requirements: Well-drained sandy loam soil rich in organic matter. Ideal pH is 6.0 to 6.5. Avoid heavy clay soils which restrict aeration.
