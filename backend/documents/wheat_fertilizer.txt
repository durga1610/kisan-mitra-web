Vegetative: Apply Urea at 45 kg/acre at crown root initiation.
Flowering: Apply DAP at 50 kg/acre and MOP at 20 kg/acre.
Fruiting: Apply Urea at 20 kg/acre or NPK 13-0-45 foliar spray.
Dosage: 45 kg/acre
Method: Top-dressing followed immediately by first or second irrigation.
Precautions: Avoid applying nitrogen during high wind conditions to prevent lodging.
