=== SUNFLOWER CROP GUIDE ===

1. Fertilizers:
Sunflower requires balanced nutrient applications. Recommended dose is 20:20:20 kg of N:P:K per acre.
Category: Oilseeds
- Apply all Phosphorus, Potassium and 50% Nitrogen as basal dressing during sowing.
- Apply remaining Nitrogen 30 days after sowing.
- Apply Gypsum or Sulphur to boost oil content and improve pod shell strength.

2. Irrigation:
Sunflower irrigation and water requirements: Low to moderate water requirement. Water at critical stages: flowering, pegging, and pod/seed filling.

3. Pest Management:
Sunflower pest management: Common pests affecting Sunflower are Aphids, Semi-looper, Caterpillar. Collect manually or spray neem seed kernel extract (NSKE 5%).

4. Growth Stages:
Sunflower has the following stage cycle durations:
- Vegetative Stage: Sowing up to 30 days.
- Flowering Stage: From 30 days to 60 days.
- Fruiting Stage: From 60 days to 90 days.
- Harvesting Stage: From 90 days onwards.

5. Harvesting:
Sunflower harvesting and post-harvest practices: For Sunflower, Harvest when leaves yellow and dry. Pull out plants, thresh, and dry pods/seeds in sun to 8% moisture.

6. Diseases:
Sunflower diseases: Common diseases affecting Sunflower are Alternaria Blight, Tikka Leaf Spot, Rust. Spray Mancozeb or Chlorothalonil to manage.

7. Soil Requirements:
Sunflower soil requirements: Requires well-drained sandy loam or clay loam soil. Optimal soil pH is 6.0 to 7.5.
