Vegetative: Apply DAP at 35 kg/acre and Urea at 20 kg/acre.
Flowering: Apply Urea at 30 kg/acre and Magnesium Sulphate at 10 kg/acre.
Fruiting: Apply Potash (MOP) at 25 kg/acre during boll development.
Dosage: 35 kg/acre
Method: Side-dressing in lines near the plants, followed by light tilling.
Precautions: Do not apply Urea during dry soil conditions without irrigation.
