=== GARLIC CROP GUIDE ===

1. Fertilizers:
Garlic requires balanced nutrient applications. Recommended dose is 50:20:25 kg of N:P:K per acre.
- Apply organic manure and full Phosphorus/Potassium as basal.
- Apply Nitrogen in two splits at 30 and 45 days after sowing.
- Sulphur application is essential to promote garlic bulb yield and flavor compounds.

2. Irrigation:
Garlic irrigation and water requirements: Moderate water requirements. Water every 8-10 days during vegetative growth. Reduce watering as bulbs mature and stop entirely 2 weeks before harvest.

3. Pest Management:
Garlic pest management: Thrips and Nematodes. Thrips suck sap from leaves causing silvering. Apply neem seed kernel extract (NSKE 5%) or dimethoate.

4. Growth Stages:
Garlic has the following stage cycle durations:
- Vegetative Stage: Sowing up to 45 days.
- Flowering Stage: From 45 days to 100 days.
- Fruiting Stage: From 100 days to 130 days.
- Harvesting Stage: From 130 days onwards.

5. Harvesting:
Garlic harvesting and post-harvest practices: Harvest when leaves turn yellow-brown and start drying. Dig bulbs carefully, cure in dry shade for a week, and store in well-ventilated dry spaces.

6. Diseases:
Garlic diseases: White Rot and Purple Blotch. White rot causes root decay with white cottony mycelium. Ensure crop rotation and use healthy seed cloves.

7. Soil Requirements:
Garlic soil requirements: Well-drained rich loamy soil with pH 6.0 to 7.0. Heavy soils lead to deformed garlic bulbs and rot.
