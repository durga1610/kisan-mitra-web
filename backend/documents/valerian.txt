=== VALERIAN CROP GUIDE ===

1. Fertilizers:
Valerian requires balanced nutrient applications. Recommended dose is 15:15:15 kg of N:P:K per acre.
Category: Medicinal Crops
- Apply all Phosphorus, Potassium and 50% Nitrogen as basal dressing.
- Apply remaining Nitrogen in monthly splits.
- Integrate organic vermicompost and farmyard manure (FYM) to maximize active compounds.

2. Irrigation:
Valerian irrigation and water requirements: Low water requirement. Highly drought-resilient. Avoid excessive irrigation, which causes root rot.

3. Pest Management:
Valerian pest management: Common pests affecting Valerian are Mites, Aphids, Whitefly. Manage by spraying neem oil (0.5%) or insecticidal soap.

4. Growth Stages:
Valerian has the following stage cycle durations:
- Vegetative Stage: Sowing up to 40 days.
- Flowering Stage: From 40 days to 80 days.
- Fruiting Stage: From 80 days to 110 days.
- Harvesting Stage: From 110 days onwards.

5. Harvesting:
Valerian harvesting and post-harvest practices: For Valerian, Harvest at peak maturity of the active part (roots, leaves, stems). Dry in shade to preserve ingredients.

6. Diseases:
Valerian diseases: Common diseases affecting Valerian are Root Rot, Seedling Blight. Avoid waterlogging and spray organic bio-fungicides (Trichoderma).

7. Soil Requirements:
Valerian soil requirements: Requires well-drained sandy loam, red loamy, or gravelly marginal soil. Optimal pH is 6.0 to 7.5.
