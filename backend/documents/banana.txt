=== BANANA CROP GUIDE ===

1. Fertilizers:
Banana requires balanced nutrient applications. Recommended dose is 110:35:140 kg of N:P:K per acre.
Category: Fruit Crops
- Apply full Phosphorus dose as basal fertilizer during planting.
- Apply Nitrogen and Potassium in 4 equal splits at 2, 4, 6, and 8 months after planting.
- Apply Zinc Sulphate (0.5%) and Ferrous Sulphate (0.2%) as foliar sprays to boost plant vigour.

2. Irrigation:
Banana irrigation and water requirements: High water requirement. Requires regular irrigation of 30-40 liters per plant per day. Provide drip irrigation. Ensure constant moisture but avoid standing water around the pseudostem.

3. Pest Management:
Banana pest management: Pseudostem Weevil and Rhizome Weevil. Weevil larvae tunnel into rootstocks and stems. Control by keeping the basin clean and applying neem cake to the soil.

4. Growth Stages:
Banana has the following stage cycle durations:
- Vegetative Stage: Sowing up to 120 days.
- Flowering Stage: From 120 days to 240 days.
- Fruiting Stage: From 240 days to 300 days.
- Harvesting Stage: From 300 days onwards.

5. Harvesting:
Banana harvesting and post-harvest practices: Harvest bunches when the ridges on the fruit surface become round and skin color turns from deep green to light green. Handle gently to prevent bruising.

6. Diseases:
Banana diseases: Panama Wilt (Fusarium oxysporum) and Sigatoka Leaf Spot. Sigatoka causes dark brown streaks on leaves. Control with propiconazole spray (1ml/L) and removing infected leaves.

7. Soil Requirements:
Banana soil requirements: Deep, well-drained loamy soil rich in organic matter. Optimal pH is 6.0 to 7.5. Extremely sensitive to soil waterlogging and high salinity.
