=== TURMERIC CROP GUIDE ===

1. Fertilizers:
Turmeric requires balanced nutrient applications. Recommended dose is 50:25:50 kg of N:P:K per acre.
Category: Spices
- Apply full Phosphorus and half Potassium as basal dose.
- Apply Nitrogen and remaining Potassium in splits at 30, 60, and 90 days.
- Apply organic mulch to retain soil moisture and reduce weeds.

2. Irrigation:
Turmeric irrigation and water requirements: High water requirement. Requires regular irrigation every 7-10 days. Very sensitive to water logging, which causes rhizome rot.

3. Pest Management:
Turmeric pest management: Rhizome Scale and Shoot Borer. Shoot borer larvae tunnel into pseudostems causing leaf drying. Spray neem oil (0.5%) to manage.

4. Growth Stages:
Turmeric has the following stage cycle durations:
- Vegetative Stage: Sowing up to 60 days.
- Flowering Stage: From 60 days to 120 days.
- Fruiting Stage: From 120 days to 200 days.
- Harvesting Stage: From 200 days onwards.

5. Harvesting:
Turmeric harvesting and post-harvest practices: Harvest when leaves turn dry and yellow-brown. Dig rhizomes carefully, boil in clean water for 45 minutes, dry in sun for 10-15 days, and polish.

6. Diseases:
Turmeric diseases: Rhizome Rot (Pythium species) and Leaf Spot. Rhizome rot causes leaves to turn yellow and roots to decay. Avoid waterlogging and drench soil with copper fungicides.

7. Soil Requirements:
Turmeric soil requirements: Sandy loam or clay loam soil rich in humus with pH 6.0 to 7.5. Requires highly porous soil to facilitate rhizome expansion.
