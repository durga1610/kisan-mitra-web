Vegetative: Apply NPK 12-32-16 at 40 kg/acre during early vegetative stage.
Flowering: Apply Urea at 35 kg/acre at knee-high and tasseling stages.
Fruiting: Apply MOP at 15 kg/acre to improve grain filling.
Dosage: 40 kg/acre
Method: Band application 5 cm away from the crop rows.
Precautions: Ensure soil has moderate moisture before applying nitrogen.
